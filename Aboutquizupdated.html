What is EarthWise

We are your trusted guide, not a store. EarthWise is the central hub where the confusion of ethical shopping ends. We do the meticulous, time-consuming research and verification so you don't have to. Our mission is to vet, verify, and curate the best sustainable and ethical brands from across the web, bringing them together in one convenient, confidence-inspiring directory.

The EarthWise Standard

Every partner we feature is vetted against strict criteria focused on three critical pillars: Environmental Impact (sustainable materials, carbon footprint reduction, waste reduction initiatives), Fair Labor & Ethics (safe working conditions, fair wages, supply chain transparency), and Product Quality & Durability (products built to last, fighting the "buy and throw away" cycle).

The Brands We Promote

We curate brands across essential lifestyle categories: Nourish (organic, fair-trade, zero-waste food that's non-toxic and nutrient-dense), Style (Slow Fashion from sustainable materials made by fairly paid workers), and Home (furniture and decor from reclaimed, recycled, or responsibly sourced materials that create non-toxic, beautiful living spaces).
